About Me

My full name is Elliott Maxwell Wheeler, but I just go by Max! While I'm currently a Full-Stack Web Developer, I was actually originally trained as a Biochemist. I received my Degree in Biochemistry with a minor in Chemistry from Louisiana State University. After working for some years as a certified Biochemist, I decided to pursue my passion for coding beyond the scope of just being a hobby.

Already having some experience with Python and MATLAB from undergraduate courses, I immediately sought out a coding course that would help me understand the intricies of Full-Stack Web Development. After much time spent committing myself to becoming better at what I do, I now find myself excited to be doing something I truly enjoy!
